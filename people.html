Principal Investigator

​    Zhixiang Lin
    Associate Professor
    Department of Statistics and Data Science
BIOGRAPHY: Zhixiang Lin received his B.S. from Tsinghua University in 2010 and Ph.D. from Yale University in 2015. During his graduate study at Yale, he was co-advised by Prof. Hongyu Zhao in the Department of Biostatistics and Prof. Matthew State in the Department of Psychiatry. Dr. Lin worked with Prof. Wing Hung Wong at Stanford University as a postdoctoral researcher from 2015 to 2018. In 2018, he was appointed as Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK).
Beyond statistics, he is a keen tennis player.
